Delete data

How to request deletion of SureKeep data.

Local data

Data stored only on your device can usually be removed by deleting receipts in the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app.

Account and synced data

If you use account or sync features, you can request deletion of account-related backend data by contacting support.

How to request deletion

Send a deletion request to: janstech.apps@gmail.com.

Use the email address associated with your SureKeep account when possible.

Business receipt notice

If you use SureKeep for business receipts or accounting material, consider applicable bookkeeping, tax, and retention obligations before deleting records.
