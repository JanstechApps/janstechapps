Delete Data

This page explains how to delete a GainsAI account in the app or request help if the in-app flow is unavailable.

Delete your account in GainsAI

In GainsAI, open Settings, choose Account and subscription, and select Delete GainsAI account. Confirm the deletion in the app. You can choose whether the GainsAI backup in Google Drive should also be deleted.

How to request deletion

If you would like to request deletion of eligible data associated with your use of GainsAI, please contact:

janstech.apps@gmail.com

Please include

  • the email subject: “GainsAI Data Deletion Request”
  • any information that helps identify your app data
  • optional screenshots or additional context if needed

What this may include

Depending on your usage, account deletion may relate to backend-stored app data such as training and nutrition profile data, workout logs, saved programs, body metrics, weekly check-ins, GainsAI Coach data, and similar app-related records.

Important notes

Account deletion is permanent. The app deletes GainsAI backend and local account data, and attempts to delete the associated Firebase Authentication user where possible.

In some cases, Firebase Authentication cleanup can require you to authenticate again. The app provides a recovery step when further action is needed to finish that cleanup.

Google Drive backup data

The in-app deletion flow can delete the GainsAI backup stored in Google Drive appDataFolder. If you do not select that option, the backup remains and may contain older account data. Google sign-in can be required to remove the backup.

Google Play subscription

Deleting a GainsAI account does not cancel a Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel an active subscription through Google Play.

Contact

For deletion requests or related privacy questions, contact: janstech.apps@gmail.com
