How GainsAI works

GainsAI keeps the planning, action, and review loop connected so the next recommendation has a basis in your own logged training.

1

Set up your direction

Use your training profile and preferences to start a program that gives your sessions a clear structure.

2

Train and log

Complete workouts and capture what happened, from working sets to the exercises you performed.

3

Review and adjust

Use progress analysis, check-ins, and Coach guidance to identify a sensible next focus for your training.

4

Keep your data available

With Pro, connect Google Drive for backup and restore under a Google account you authorise separately.

GainsAI Coach

GainsAI Coach uses the app’s own backend, your current training context, and deterministic coaching logic to explain program, progress, and training-priority signals.

It is built to help you understand possible next actions. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace a doctor, physiotherapist, dietitian, personal trainer, or other healthcare professional.

Adaptive, not absolute

Recommendations can draw attention to exercise progress, training consistency, recovery check-ins, and body-trend context. They are suggestions to assess, not promises of a result.

Training decisions remain yours. Use your judgement and seek qualified professional advice when it is appropriate for your circumstances.

Privacy and account controls

GainsAI uses authenticated backend services for account-linked features. Google sign-in, Firebase Authentication, the backend, and Google Drive are used only for the functions you choose to use.

You can delete a GainsAI account from the app. The flow covers backend and local account data, can include the GainsAI Drive backup, and attempts Firebase Authentication cleanup where possible. Deleting an account does not cancel a Google Play subscription.

Google Drive backup and restore

Pro includes Google Drive backup and restore. Drive is authorised separately, and backups are stored in Google Drive’s app-specific storage area.

Before replacing an existing backup, the app can ask you to review recovery and account-mismatch information. This helps you make an informed choice when changing device or account.

Frequently asked questions

Does GainsAI replace a healthcare or fitness professional?

No. GainsAI is a fitness and wellness tool. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency assistance, or professional healthcare advice.

How is Pro access handled?

Purchases are made through Google Play. The app sends a purchase for secure backend verification, then shows the backend-confirmed Pro entitlement. You can restore purchases and manage a Google Play subscription through the app or Google Play.

Can I back up or restore my data?

Yes, GainsAI Pro includes Google Drive backup and restore after you authorise a Google account for Drive. Drive connection is separate from your GainsAI sign-in.

How can I delete my account and data?

Use the in-app account deletion flow or review the deletion instructions. The app can delete account data and optionally remove the GainsAI backup from Google Drive; a subscription must be managed separately in Google Play.
